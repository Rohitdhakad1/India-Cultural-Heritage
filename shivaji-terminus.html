Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus

Architectural Marvel of Mumbai

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (CSMT) is one of Mumbai's most iconic landmarks and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. This magnificent railway station is a fusion of Victorian Gothic Revival architecture and Indian traditional styles.

Built in 1887, CSMT is not only a busy railway hub but also a symbol of Mumbai’s colonial past and architectural brilliance.

Facade at Night

The building lights up beautifully in the evening, showing off its arches, domes, and spires in stunning detail.

Gothic Architecture

Detailed carvings, turrets, and stained glass windows blend Indian craftsmanship with Gothic influences.

Historic Interior

The interiors maintain a grand feel with high ceilings, old chandeliers, and bustling energy of daily commuters.
