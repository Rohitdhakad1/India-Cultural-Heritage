Bandipur National Park

Wildlife Treasure of Karnataka

Bandipur National Park is a renowned wildlife sanctuary in Karnataka, part of the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve. It is famous for its biodiversity and is home to tigers, elephants, leopards, and many rare bird species.

The park offers jungle safaris, lush forest trails, and a chance to experience India's rich natural heritage up close.

Elephant Corridor

Bandipur's protected elephant corridors support large herds in their natural habitat, offering amazing sighting opportunities.

Jungle Safari

Guided safaris allow visitors to explore the park’s landscape while spotting wildlife in their natural environment.

Sunset View

Evenings in Bandipur offer breathtaking sunset views over the dense forest canopy, full of chirping birds and wildlife sounds.
