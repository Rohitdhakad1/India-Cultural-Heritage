Chhattisgarh

Cultural Heritage of Chhattisgarh

Chhattisgarh is known for its deep-rooted tribal traditions, folk music, colorful dances, and intricate handicrafts. The region's tribal communities preserve ancient customs, festivals, and rituals passed down for generations.

The state is famous for traditional dance forms like Panthi and Raut Nacha, terracotta art, bell metal crafts, and natural attractions like Chitrakote Falls. Bastar Dussehra is a unique festival celebrated with great fervor, distinct from mainstream Dussehra.

Tribal Dance

Traditional folk dances like Panthi and Raut Nacha represent the cultural spirit of the tribal communities.

Chitrakote Falls

Often called the Niagara Falls of India, this waterfall is a natural wonder and a spiritual site for locals.

Bastar Art

Renowned for its bell metal (Dhokra) craft, Bastar artisans create intricate tribal figurines and decorative pieces.
