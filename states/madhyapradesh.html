Madhya Pradesh

Cultural Heritage of Madhya Pradesh

Often called the "Heart of India", Madhya Pradesh boasts a rich cultural and historical legacy. It is home to the magnificent Khajuraho temples, Buddhist monuments at Sanchi, and majestic forts like Gwalior. The state is also known for its tribal culture, classical music, and art forms such as Gond painting.

Traditional festivals, vibrant folk dances like Rai and Grida, and crafts like Chanderi and Maheshwari weaving highlight the state’s diverse cultural landscape.

Khajuraho Temples

Famous for their intricate sculptures and erotic carvings, showcasing ancient Indian art and architecture.

Sanchi Stupa

A UNESCO World Heritage Site representing Buddhist art, with finely carved gateways and peaceful ambiance.

Gond Tribal Art

Colorful folk art that tells stories through nature-inspired motifs, practiced by the Gond community.
