Cultural Heritage of Odisha

Odisha, known for its ancient temples, classical dance forms like Odissi, and intricate art and craft, reflects a deeply spiritual and artistic heritage. The Konark Sun Temple and Jagannath Temple in Puri are iconic landmarks that draw pilgrims and tourists alike.

The state is also famous for its vibrant festivals, handloom traditions like Sambalpuri textiles, Pattachitra paintings, and tribal cultures that enrich its diversity.

Konark Sun Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this 13th-century temple is a marvel of architecture and sculpture, shaped like a chariot of the Sun God.

Odissi Dance

One of the oldest classical dance forms in India, known for its fluid movements, spiritual themes, and graceful expressions.

Rath Yatra

The grand chariot festival of Lord Jagannath held annually in Puri, attracting millions of devotees and tourists from around the world.
