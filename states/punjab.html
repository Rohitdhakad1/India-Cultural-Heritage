Punjab

Cultural Heritage of Punjab

Punjab, the land of five rivers, is known for its vibrant culture, energetic dances, and rich traditions. The state is a symbol of bravery, hospitality, and celebration. It’s home to the Golden Temple in Amritsar, one of the most sacred sites in Sikhism.

Punjab's folk music, colorful festivals like Baisakhi, traditional attire like phulkari, and delicious cuisine such as makki di roti and sarson da saag reflect the joyous spirit of its people.

Golden Temple

Also known as Harmandir Sahib, it is the most important pilgrimage site for Sikhs, showcasing peace and spiritual harmony.

Bhangra Dance

A high-energy traditional dance form that represents the agricultural spirit and festive joy of Punjab.

Baisakhi Festival

Celebrated as the harvest festival and Sikh New Year, Baisakhi is marked with fairs, dances, and prayers.
