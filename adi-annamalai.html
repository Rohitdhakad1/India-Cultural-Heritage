Adi Annamalai Temple

Spiritual Significance of Adi Annamalai

Located near Tiruvannamalai, Adi Annamalai Temple is an ancient Shiva shrine steeped in sacred history. It is believed to be older than the renowned Arunachaleswara Temple and holds a special place in Tamil Saivism.

The temple is surrounded by a peaceful environment ideal for spiritual seekers and pilgrims, offering a glimpse into the devotional traditions of South India.

Temple Entrance

The entrance is simple and serene, welcoming devotees and visitors into a sacred atmosphere.

Inner Sanctum

The sanctum sanctorum houses the Shiva Lingam and radiates divine energy during daily rituals.

Temple Architecture

Although modest, the architecture reflects South Indian temple styles, with intricate stone carvings and deep spiritual aura.
