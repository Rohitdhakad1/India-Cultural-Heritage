Cultural Heritage of Bangalore

Bangalore, also known as Bengaluru, is the capital of Karnataka and is famously called the "Silicon Valley of India". Known for its pleasant weather, parks, and IT hubs, it also houses historical landmarks that reflect the city's cultural richness.
